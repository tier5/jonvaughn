Who is Jon Vaughn?

I am a well-rounded web developer. I am a senior programmer with sound knowledge of front-end methods. I love the system and order and personify quality. I love spending time fixing the minor details and optimizing the web applications. I also like working in a team, where I believe you will learn more and also faster. I believe in the saying" two heads are better than one".
I am able to work efficiently on my own or with a team. I have the ability to lead small teams and mentor other workers. I am proud of myself being able to utilize my knowledge and initiative to render top quality results within fixed deadlines. I bask a challenge and look for opportunities for growth and honing my skill set.
Some people call what I do interactive design, some call it web design, some people believe I am a Front-end developer, others consider me a UI designer. I see what I do as a blend of all these things and eventually don't think it truly matters which label you love to use.
The thing that matters is the outcome of my work, which is always emphasized at the point where business, user and technical necessities are fulfilled and ultimately becomes an ideal product.
I am Jon Vaughn, an IT developer and surely a tech professional. This website is about me, my work and what I am all about. I am sure you will love this place!
Some of my favorite tech interests are WordPress, JavaScript, PHP, Git, but usually I love everything about programming. Actually, I am working as a CEO of my company Tier5 Technologies, based in Indiana, USA.
This is not only my personal website, but also a demonstration of my programming skills, where I test code and things that will stun you... hence if you notice any problem, you can discuss with me before deciding to hire me.
I grew up in Indiana. I have travelled a bit, but mostly stay within the USA. I develop websites, but I am also passionate about the facets of design. I mainly code in JavaScript, CSS, HTML and Git, but also smatter in PHP. I have ample experience in WordPress theme creation. In simple words, I am a problem solver, and love coding issues to be fixed while thinking logically through every barrier faced whilst developing a website. I also love music and play-station games.
