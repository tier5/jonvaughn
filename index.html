Who is Jon Vaughn?

My name is Jon Vaughn and I am from Indiana, USA. I have more than 5 years of experience in software and web development. I am educated at Indiana University in the field of Computer Engineering. I am presently implementing my programing skills into the web development projects of my company. Specifically, I am mainly interested in concurrent data structures. My goal is to better understand the complexities of synchronization. I am a CEO of a major web development company. I love programming and this is what my work is all about.
I warmly welcome you to my website. This is my little home in the cyberspace where I am shamelessly promoting myself, yet personally and professionally.
My professional area of proficiency is web and software development. You can go through my notable projects mentioned in the portfolio section of this website. I love to work as a web developer. I specialize in the development of websites that enhance usability and accessibility by conforming to current web standards. Occasionally, I also look for ways to integrate Flash into my websites, while still maintaining usability and accessibility.
I always wanted to be an engineer, I just don't have any idea what it is like to be one. According to me, engineers were the people who make things work, but I don't have any idea how, and I used to believe mechanical engineers as the only type of engineer. Once I made up my mind that I wanted to be an engineer, I had to decide what type of engineer I wanted to become. I first leaned towards electrical and mechanical because that was the image of an engineer in my mind-making things work with tools, building things, and working with physical tools. Then I started studying about computer science.
Why Computer science?
I got admission in Indiana University knowing that I wanted to be a computer science alumni, due to a few reasons. My idea was to create anything I want and not be restricted by physics or materials or any other norms that will govern the design of things. The concept of application of things and testing may not be possible in any other way. This is one of the few reasons why I decided to be a Computer Science graduate. My passion for computes paved my way to be the current CEO of Tier5 Technologies, a web and software development company. Hire me today!
