Who is Jon Vaughn?

Hi! My name is Jon Vaughn, and I am presently pursuing education in the fields of neuroscience, mathematical theory and computer engineering. Being trained at the Indiana University in the field of computer engineering, while my classmates were graduating and getting jobs, I started my first company and earned by very first million from my own complex statistical software. I later founded Tier5 Technologies and commenced building its initial team.I warmly welcome you to my personal website.I am the CEO of a reputable web development company.
I have been developing software for more than 5 years. I have command over almost every programming language and all the major development technologies in the field of software development on a wide range of intricacies. You can go through some of my favorite projects on this website. My work at Tier5 basically comprised of developing software. I also developed API samples which were deployed to customers, bettered an existing web development system with the implementation of GIT technology. In my spare time, I love playing play station games. I am passionate about leading teams to create website applications and software for ascendability, dependability and front-end usability. I blend my technical competency with excitement and talent to transform talented people into expert engineers.
I love programming! My pervasive computing work emphasizes on developing prototype techniques and tools for interdisciplinary teams. Along with my research, I have also worked with many fresh computer engineering graduates. The aim of this website is to tell you something about myself and to give you a sneak preview of what I have accomplished till date, right from my undergraduate days at the Indiana University and things I love to do on my own personal time.
To start with, let me tell you something about myself. My computer science program emphasized in the practical software design, but undoubtedly, I still have to endure all the theoretical proficiency one would expect from a computer engineering program from Indiana University. Although I have completed the necessary mathematics courses necessary for the computer science program, I have opted for the computer engineering program as it undeniably reflects my abilities and my interests. Unfortunately, I am not the theoretical researcher like a computer scientist, but instead a more practical one who wants to learn new techniques of developing website and software and thereby honing my software development skills. Therefore the computer engineering field fits me perfectly!
