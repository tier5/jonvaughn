Who is Jon Vaughn?

"Hi! My name is Jon Vaughn. I am a graphic artist specializing in the design of brand logos. Self-employed for more than 5 years, I work from my office in Indiana, USA, where it is my pleasure to design for brands of all sizes all across the world. My design horizons have really opened up and I am ready to meet the new challenges that God has in store for me ahead! I am a graphic artist, born and raised in the USA. I work for my own company Tier5 Technologies. I have 5+ years of experience in working with different kinds of clients.I consider myself a creative master and always ready to face new challenges in my workplace. I am always available for freelance work. For any projects, please feel free to get in touch with me after browsing through my portfolio where you may get a wide range of chosen designs and artworks. I am always available for collaborations and commissions. I want the online presence of all my clients to be awesome and generally I allow my design to speak for itself. I just love music, technology and many other things. In simple words, I am a design-aholic and a highly motivated user experience designer, graphic artist and creative director. I just love what I do! My journey of becoming a tech savvy, started at a very young age when I decided to become a computer engineer.
My natural instinct to create beautiful things, led me to believe that I am going to be an engineer. Nonetheless, I was missing an integral part of being an engineer- I don't have any idea what kind of engineer I am going to become. But my inner voice said "yes" and eventually I decided to become a computer engineer. Design is now an integral part of my life since graduating from the Indiana University. I love working with new clients, and making new business relationships. So.....Can we collaborate?"
Jon Vaughn is an USA based designer specializing in branding, graphic design, and UI/web design. He has a clientele all around the world and his work is highly admired. Fueled by curiosity, Jon finds himself in a process of continuous experimentation and iteration. His work is really sophisticated and consistent. His goal is to draw the viewer to go through his perception, displaying intricacy within simple layouts.He is the CEO of Tier5 Technologies based in Indiana, USA.
